Sample Water Damage Claim — Tom & Sarah Rivera
===============================================

Agent:
Thank you for calling Sample Insurance Company, this is Alex. May I have your name and policy number?

Tom Rivera:
Hi there! This is Tom Rivera, policy number SM-4471. My wife Sarah is here with me — she's the one who discovered the mess.

[HANDOFF: Tom -> Sarah]

Sarah Rivera:
Don't let him downplay it — I came home to a waterfall in the kitchen!

Agent:
Oh my! I can hear this is serious. Let me verify Mr. Rivera's identity first. Mr. Rivera, can I get your full legal name?

[HANDOFF: Sarah -> Tom]

Tom Rivera:
Thomas James Rivera. But everyone calls me Tom.

Agent:
Thank you. And your date of birth and the last four of your Social?

Tom Rivera:
March 15, 1978. Last four is 6234.

Agent:
Great, and your address and a good callback number?

Tom Rivera:
42 Maple Lane, Hartford, CT 06105. Phone is 860-555-0147.

Agent:
Perfect, you're verified. Thank you, Mr. Rivera. Do I have your permission to speak with Mrs. Rivera on this recorded line?

Tom Rivera:
Absolutely, go right ahead. She knows way more about what happened than I do — I was at school when the pipe went!

Agent:
Thank you. Sarah, can you walk me through what happened?

[HANDOFF: Tom -> Sarah]

Sarah Rivera:
So last Tuesday I came home from work and found the upstairs bathroom pipe had burst. Water soaked through the ceiling into the kitchen below. The kitchen ceiling has a big water stain and it's actually sagging now. The bathroom floor tiles are completely warped.

Agent:
That sounds significant. Did you have any emergency repairs done?

Sarah Rivera:
Yes, we called a plumber right away. He did an emergency repair to stop the leak — that cost us $350. I have the receipt and photos of everything.

Agent:
Good, you'll want to hold onto those. I can start a claim for you. Your deductible on this policy is $500, and the plumber's $350 receipt can be applied toward that.

Sarah Rivera:
Oh good. So what's the process from here? How long does this usually take?

Agent:
We'll assign an adjuster who will contact you within 48 hours to schedule an inspection. After they assess the damage, we'll provide an estimate. The whole process typically takes 2 to 3 weeks depending on the extent of the repairs.

Sarah Rivera:
Okay. One more thing — can we do temporary repairs to the kitchen ceiling while we wait? It's sagging and I'm worried it could collapse.

Agent:
Yes, absolutely. You can do temporary repairs to prevent further damage — that's actually encouraged. Just keep receipts for everything and take photos before and after. Those costs will be part of your claim.

Sarah Rivera:
That's a relief. Thank you, that covers my questions.

Agent:
You're welcome. Is there anything else I can help you or Mr. Rivera with today?

Sarah Rivera:
Tom, anything else? ... No, I think we're good. Thank you, Alex!

Agent:
Thank you both for calling Sample Insurance Company. I hope the repairs go smoothly. Have a great day!
